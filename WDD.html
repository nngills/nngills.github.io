ds-items

An item guide for the game, Don't Starve by Klei Entertainment. The site was made as a tool to help players understand the various components of an item. It was created for my final at Full Sail University and was coded in PHP, HTML, CSS and JQuery. I used mySQL to populate a database with over 1,000 items parsed from the game's files. The site was shared between members of the Don't Starve community and was very popular on the game's subreddit.
